FAQ

Questions We Always Hear

Is the demo really free?
Yes. We build you a fully live, working website before any payment is made. You see it, you share it, you tell us what changes you want. You only pay when you decide to keep it and go live. No hidden conditions.
How long does the demo take?
Most demos are ready within 48 hours of us receiving your brief. For more complex projects it may take up to 72 hours. Either way, you'll get a shareable link you can open on any device.
What if I want changes after seeing the demo?
Tell us exactly what you want changed and we fix it. Colors, layout, text, sections, features. We iterate with you until it feels right. Revisions are part of the process, not a premium add-on.
Where will my site be hosted?
On Cloudflare Pages, one of the fastest and most reliable hosting platforms in the world. Your site loads from data centers close to your visitors, giving it near-instant load times globally. And it's free to host there.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
Absolutely none. You describe what you need, we handle everything. Design, code, hosting, domain setup, the whole thing. You just need to be able to tell us what your business does and what you like.
Can I get a custom domain?
Yes. We can connect your existing domain or help you get a new one. Whether it's a .com, .in, or anything else, we'll handle the setup and make sure everything points correctly.
